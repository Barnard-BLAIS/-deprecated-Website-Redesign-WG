The Barnard Library and Academic Information Services (BLAIS) unit continues to advance the mission of the College through the development of collections and information resources, increasingly expanding its support for excellence and innovation in teaching and learning. This service-rich organization supports a wide range of curricular and co-curricular activities.

We envision our services and our spaces as a catalyst for knowledge creation and investigation - a vibrant social and intellectual center that brings scholars and disciplines together. Our unique collections not only stimulate the intellectual curiosity of our community, but elevate Barnard's reputation as a distinguished center for research.
